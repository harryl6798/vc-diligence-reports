# GPTZero: Master VC Diligence Questionnaire (Phase 8)

Based on our recursive diligence, the following 20 questions are prioritized for the founding team to validate the core investment thesis and mitigate identifying risks.

### Category A: Technology & AI Moat
1.  **Hallucination Management**: "How do you handle 'hallucinations' in high-stakes legal or medical verification where accuracy is 🔴 CRITICAL?"
2.  **Model Agnosticism**: "What is your strategy for model-agnosticism? How easily can your 7-layer pipeline adapt to new 'reasoning' models like OpenAI's o1 or future GPT-5 releases?"
3.  **Bypass Arms Race**: "As 'humanizing' tools evolve to inject synthetic noise into LLM outputs, what is the next frontier for the GPTZero Shield adversarial defense layer?"
4.  **Telemetry Data**: "Can you provide data on the accuracy gains achieved by incorporating typing telemetry (GPTZero Docs) vs. static text analysis?"

### Category B: Commercial & Unit Economics
5.  **Data Licensing**: "Walk us through the marginal cost and revenue potential of licensing your 600M+ document database to frontier AI labs. What is the typical deal structure?"
6.  **Enterprise Sales**: "With the AFT partnership as a wedge, what is your 'Expansion' path into district-wide or state-wide educational contracts?"
7.  **Customer ROI**: "Who is the 'Economic Buyer' in your enterprise deals, and what is the specific ROI metric they use to justify the license (e.g., insurance premium reduction, compliance cost savings)?"
8.  **Churn Defense**: "How do you defend against churn from schools that decide to 'embrace' AI and stop policing its use?"

### Category C: Market & Competitive
9.  **Turnitin Displacement**: "What is your specific win-rate when competing directly against Turnitin for institutional contracts, and what is the #1 reason they switch to GPTZero?"
10. **Platform Risk**: "OpenAI recently sunset their detector. If they were to release a 'verified' watermark or internal detection API tomorrow, how does GPTZero's browser-level telemetry maintain its moat?"
11. **Grammarly Competition**: "Grammarly is moving into authorship verification. How do you differentiate GPTZero Docs from Grammarly's internal writing replay features?"

### Category D: Team & Culture
12. **Technical Alpha**: "How has Alex Cui’s background in autonomous driving (perception/motion forecasting) uniquely informed the development of GPTZero’s multi-task detection architecture?"
13. **Talent Pipeline**: "How do you attract 'A-tier' researchers from CMU or the Vector Institute when competing against frontier labs that can offer $500k+ salaries?"
14. **Mission Drift**: "How do you balance the 'policing' needs of educators with your stated mission to 'preserve humanity' and maintain student goodwill?"

### Category E: Legal, Regulatory & Governance
15. **ESL Bias**: "Provide the latest internal benchmarks on false positive rates for non-native English speakers. How do you ensure the 'Education Module' remains equitable?"
16. **Data Privacy**: "What is your posture on student data privacy (FERPA/GDPR)? Is student work used to train your models, and can institutions opt-out into a sovereign silo?"
17. **IP Provenance**: "Do you have clear ownership of all IP developed during Edward Tian's senior thesis phase at Princeton?"
18. **Hallucination Liability**: "What is the legal liability framework if GPTZero fails to detect a hallucination that leads to a material error in a professional setting?"

### Category F: The "Killer" Questions
19. **Scale Limit**: "What is the single biggest technical constraint preventing GPTZero from scanning the entire 'live' internet for synthetic content in real-time?"
20. **Exit Strategy**: "Given the Turnitin $1.75B comp, do you view GPTZero as a standalone public company or a strategic asset for a broader 'AI Safety' or 'Enterprise Governance' platform?"
