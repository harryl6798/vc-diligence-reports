# GPTZero - Wave 9: Legal, Regulatory & Governance Audit

## IP Provenance
- **Ownership**: GPTZero Inc. owns the core intellectual property. Edward Tian's initial model was developed as a senior thesis at Princeton.
- **Risk**: Potential "University IP Overhang" is low given the tool was launched independently and subsequently incorporated.
- **Patents**: Alex Cui holds 3 patents related to human-AI interaction, which are likely assigned to GPTZero Inc.

## Data Privacy & Compliance
- **FERPA**: GPTZero is compliant with the Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act (FERPA), which is mandatory for US educational institutional contracts.
- **GDPR**: Maintains a privacy policy addressing EU data protection requirements.
- **Data Usage**: Explicitly states that student data is not used to train external models without consent (critical for academic institutional trust).

## Regulatory Risks
- **ESL Bias**: A primary regulatory and ethical concern is the higher false-positive rate for non-native English speakers. GPTZero has mitigated this through a specialized "Education Module" and has outperformed Turnitin in independent audits regarding ESL bias.
- **Misuse Liability**: There is a risk of legal action from students wrongfully accused of cheating. GPTZero mitigates this by positioning its tool as a "guide" rather than a final verdict and providing the "Writing Report" as evidence for the student.

## Governance
- **Board**: Includes representatives from Footwork VC and Uncork Capital.
- **Advisors**: Includes former CEOs of Reuters and The New York Times, providing strong governance in the media and disinformation space.
