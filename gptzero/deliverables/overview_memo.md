# Investment Memorandum: GPTZero Inc.
![GPTZero Logo](../assets/og_image.png)

**Date**: March 15, 2026
**Stage**: Series A+ (Expansion)
**Recommendation**: **OVERWEIGHT / HIGH CONVICTION**

## Executive Summary
GPTZero has successfully transitioned from a viral academic detector into the "Verification Layer for the Internet." By achieving profitability within 18 months and scaling ARR from ~$7M to an estimated ~$24M in 2025 (250% YoY), the company has demonstrated rare capital efficiency and product-market fit. Their strategic pivot from static detection ("Product") to authorship provenance ("Process") via GPTZero Docs creates a defensible technical moat that is highly resistant to standard AI "humanizing" bypasses.

## Key Investment Highlights
- **Founder-Market Fit**: Edward Tian (Princeton CS/Journalism) provides the narrative alpha needed to lead the "AI transparency" movement, while Alex Cui (UofT AI Research) anchors the technical moat.
- **The "Authorship" Moat**: Unlike competitors who rely on static text analysis, GPTZero Docs tracks typing telemetry and revision history, creating a browser-level data moat.
- **Enterprise Scale**: The 1.8M-member AFT partnership and the pivot to licensing "ground truth" human data to AI labs transform GPTZero from a teacher tool into a critical infrastructure provider.
- **Category Definition**: As "Model Collapse" (AI models training on AI data) becomes a systemic risk for frontier labs, GPTZero's 600M+ verified human document database becomes a high-margin strategic asset.

## Recommendation
We recommend an **OVERWEIGHT** position. GPTZero is the clear category leader in a winner-take-most market. Their trajectory mirrors the early days of Turnitin ($1.75B exit) but with 10x higher growth velocity and a broader enterprise/data-licensing mandate.
