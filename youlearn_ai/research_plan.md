# Research Plan: YouLearn.ai Due Diligence

## Core Investment Hypothesis
YouLearn.ai aims to disrupt the personalized education market by using AI to create autonomous learning paths and content synthesis, capturing a significant portion of the lifelong learning and corporate upskilling sectors.

## The "Kill the Deal" Questions
1. Is the technology a thin wrapper around generic LLMs, or is there a proprietary reasoning/data engine?
2. How does the company handle copyright and IP for synthesized educational content?
3. What is the actual user retention and "Time-to-Value" compared to incumbents like Coursera or Duolingo?

## Research Waves
- **Wave 1 (Identity)**: General launch info, YC status, funding history, and leadership team.
- **Wave 2 (Alpha)**: Founder backgrounds and technical pedigree.
- **Wave 3 (Architecture)**: Underlying models, data strategy, and integration hooks.
- **Wave 4 (Market)**: Competitive matrix, ARR benchmarks, and 2026 AI impact analysis.
