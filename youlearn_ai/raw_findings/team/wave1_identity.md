# Findings: YouLearn.ai Wave 1 - Identity & Traction

## Core Identity
- **Company**: YouLearn.ai (also referred to as YouLearn).
- **Headquarters**: San Francisco, CA.
- **Stage**: Seed (YC Spring 2025 / S25).
- **Total Funding**: ~$504,000 (Primary round led by Y Combinator).

## Leadership Team
- **David Yu (CEO)**: Educational content creator (200M+ views), strong GTM/marketing background.
- **Achyut Krishna Byanjankar (Co-founder)**: Technical lead, created 6 AI apps in 6 weeks, built "Apollo" (featured by LangChain/Vercel).
- **Soami Kapadia (Co-founder)**: Technical lead, interned at Groq, high-performance computing background.

## Key Traction Metrics (Late 2025)
- **ARR**: ~$840,000 (Based on $70,000 MRR).
- **User Base**: 150,000+ Monthly Active Users (MAU).
- **Usage**: 2.2 million+ materials uploaded (PDFs, YouTube, Lecture recordings).
- **Growth Velocity**: Scaled from $1k to $70k MRR in 8 months.

## Strategic Position
YouLearn.ai utilizes a "Drop & Learn" interface to generate "Source-Grounded" AI tutors. This positioning directly addresses the hallucination problem in AI education by restricting the AI's knowledge base to specific uploaded documents.
