# Findings: YouLearn.ai - Legal, Regulatory & Governance

## 1. Severity Audit
- **🔴 CRITICAL**: **Content IP**. Synthesizing proprietary textbooks could trigger DMCA/Copyright claims from publishers (Pearson/McGraw Hill). Mitigation: Safe Harbor compliance and User-Generated Content (UGC) legal protections.
- **🟠 MATERIAL**: **FERPA/COPPA**. Student data handling requires strict SOC 2 Type II and privacy shielding. 
- **🟠 MATERIAL**: **IP Assignment**. Formal waiver needed from Groq regarding the MoA framework code.

## 2. Corporate Governance
- **Status**: Delaware C-Corp (Assumed).
- **Elections**: 83(b) filings must be verified for all 3 founders.
- **Vesting**: 4-year standard with 1-year cliff should be confirmed for early employees.
