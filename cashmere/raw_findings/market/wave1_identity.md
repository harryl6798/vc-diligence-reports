# Cashmere.io - Wave 1: Identity & Early History

## Core Identity
- **Company Name**: Cashmere.io (Vandal AI, Inc.)
- **Location**: Salt Lake City, Utah
- **Mission**: To provide the "commercial and technical rails" for the publishing industry to navigate the AI economy. It acts as the infrastructure layer between premium publishers and AI models (LLMs).
- **Core Product**: A platform that enables publishers to license, monetize, and protect their content while allowing AI agents (like Perplexity or ChatGPT) to access and cite it accurately.

## Funding History
- **Seed Round (January/February 2026)**: $5 million led by **Reach Capital**.
  - **Participating Investors**: Founders Future, Fortitude Ventures, Lightshed Ventures, Ingram Content Group, Naver Corp (parent of Wattpad), and Pearson Publishing.
- **Pre-Seed Round (May 2025)**: $1 million backed by the **Perplexity Fund** and **F7 Ventures**.
- **Strategic Note**: Perplexity is not just an investor but also a primary client, using Cashmere to handle premium data integrations.

## Key Clients & Partners
- **AI Innovators**: Perplexity.
- **Premium Publishers**: John Wiley & Sons, Harvard Business Publishing.

## Value Proposition
- **IP Protection**: Prevents unauthorized scraping via a "licensed discovery gateway."
- **Inference-Based Monetization**: Shifts from one-time "bulk" training deals to metered, per-use (per-inference) pricing.
- **BYOL (Bring Your Own License)**: Allows existing subscribers to access premium content within third-party AI tools.
- **Omnipub**: A proprietary knowledge graph format for turning books/journals into AI-native data.
