# Findings: Clinical Impact and News - Mecha Health

## Strategic Partnerships
### AmeriRad Radiology Solutions
In early 2025, Mecha Health partnered with AmeriRad to integrate their AI into teleradiology workflows.
- **Goal:** Move from reading one scan per hour to one scan every five minutes for high-volume studies.
- **Focus:** Combating radiologist burnout and reducing reporting delays in large-scale teleradiology networks.

## Clinical Performance
- **Critical Error Rate:** MechaNet XR has a critical error rate of approximately **4.6%**, compared to a human radiologist rate of **3.0%**. This small gap positions it as a highly effective diagnostic assistant that still requires human oversight.
- **Workflow Speed:** Processing occurs in sub-seconds, with draft reports ready instantly.
- **Interpretability:** The use of SAE-Rad allows for "trust but verify" workflows where physicians can see the evidence for AI-suggested findings.
