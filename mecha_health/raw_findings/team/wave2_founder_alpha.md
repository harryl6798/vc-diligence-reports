# Mecha Health - Wave 2: Founder Alpha & Team Audit

## The Founders: "Bridge Talent" (Clinical + Deep ML)
The founding team consists of 4 individuals who uniquely bridge the gap between clinical medical practice and frontier machine learning research.

### 1. Dr. Ahmed Abdulaal (CEO & Co-founder)
- **Background**: Medical Doctor (trained at Imperial College London) and PhD candidate in Computer Science at University College London (UCL) as a Microsoft PhD Scholar.
- **The 'Spike'**: Deep clinical domain expertise combined with high-level AI research. He previously worked as a Research Scientist at AstraZeneca's Center for AI within their foundation modeling team.
- **Research Prowess**: Over 20 peer-reviewed papers in top-tier venues (ICLR, NeurIPS, ICML). His research focuses heavily on **causal modeling agents** and **generative image modeling**. During COVID-19, he used AI to discover the link between loss of smell and infection, influencing UK policy.

### 2. Nina Montaña Brown (CTO & Co-founder)
- **Background**: PhD candidate at UCL within the Wellcome / EPSRC Centre for Interventional and Surgical Sciences (WEISS) and the Centre for Medical Image Computing (CMIC).
- **The 'Spike'**: Surgical vision, medical image registration, and deploying high-performance ML algorithms in clinical settings.
- **Previous Roles**: Machine Learning Researcher at Verdure Imaging (2020-2023) and Research Intern at Odin Vision (acquired by Olympus). She also has expertise in navigating CE and FDA regulatory documentation for medical AI.

### 3. Ayodeji (Deji) Ijishakin (COO & Co-founder)
- **Background**: PhD candidate in Machine Learning and Medical Imaging at UCL EPSRC Centre for Doctoral Training.
- **The 'Spike'**: Generative modeling (diffusion models), representation learning, and interpretability. Notably authored research on using sparse autoencoders for interpretable radiology report generation ("An X-ray is worth 15 features").
- **Ecosystem Alpha**: Founded and led the London Founders Club, building a strong network in the UK startup ecosystem.

### 4. Hugo Fry (Chief Scientific Officer & Co-founder)
- **The 'Spike'**: Active researcher in medical AI and foundation models, specifically focusing on the accuracy, interpretability, and "inductive bias" of AI. He has co-authored research evaluating the performance of AI models (including Mecha Net) against complex ICU cases and GPT-4V.

## Talent Density & Cohesion
- **Cohesion**: The team is deeply intertwined through UCL's elite medical imaging and NLP labs. The shared academic rigor and co-authorship history provide strong evidence of team cohesion.
- **Density**: The company maintains a near 100% technical/clinical density. They are hiring researchers from top AI labs (OpenAI, DeepMind, Vector Institute) rather than expanding a generic sales org, indicating a commitment to maintaining a massive technical moat.
