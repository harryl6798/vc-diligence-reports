# Mecha Health - Wave 1: Identity, Trajectory & Capital Structure

## Corporate Identity
- **Legal Name**: Mecha Health Inc. (registered as a Delaware C-Corp)
- **Founding Date**: Late 2023 / Early 2024.
- **Headquarters**: San Francisco, CA (with team members affiliated with UK institutions like UCL and Imperial College).
- **Mission**: To build next-generation vision-language foundation models for "superhuman radiology" and automate medical reporting to reduce burnout.

## Capital Structure & Funding History
- **Y Combinator Cohort**: Accepted into the **Winter 2025 (W25)** batch (Note: some sources previously cited S24, but W25 is the most current and official listing).
- **Seed Round**: Raised **$4.1 million** in November 2025.
- **Lead Investor**: **Valia Ventures**.
- **Participating Investors**: Y Combinator, **Reach Capital**, **Phosphor Capital**, and **Rebel Fund**.
- **Burn & Efficiency**: Operating with an extremely lean team (4-5 core members) post-funding, indicating a low burn multiple and high capital efficiency focused entirely on R&D and pilot deployments rather than massive GTM scaling.

## Growth Velocity & Headcount
- **Headcount**: ~4-5 employees as of early 2026 (primarily the founding team).
- **Departmental Focus**: Almost exclusively Engineering, ML Research, and Clinical validation. There is no evidence of a large dedicated sales force; current enterprise pilots (like AmeriRad) are likely founder-led sales.
- **Traction Proxy**: Has already secured partnerships with "the largest privately owned radiology practice in the US" and a multinational teleradiology firm (AmeriRad).
