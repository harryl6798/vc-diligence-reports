# Findings: Company Overview - Mecha Health

## Overview
Mecha Health is an applied AI lab specializing in foundation models for radiology. Founded in 2024 and headquartered in San Francisco, the company is a member of the **Y Combinator Winter 2025** batch. They recently raised a **$4.6M seed round** to scale their "superhuman" radiology tools.

## Mission
To **amplify human expertise** in radiology. Mecha Health aims to address the global shortage of radiologists and the resulting burnout by automating repetitive tasks and providing high-fidelity diagnostic drafts, enabling doctors to deliver care at a much larger scale.

## Founding Team
The team consists of medical doctors and machine learning researchers from top UK institutions:
- **Ahmed Abdulaal (CEO):** MD (Imperial), PhD Candidate (UCL/Microsoft), former Research Scientist at AstraZeneca. Published 20+ papers in ICLR/NeurIPS.
- **Nina Montaña Brown (CTO):** PhD in AI for surgical vision (UCL). Expert in FDA/CE-marked medical devices.
- **Ayodeji Ijishakin (COO):** PhD Candidate in ML and Medical Imaging (UCL). Research focused on interpretability and diffusion models.
- **Hugo Fry (CSO):** Mathematics/Physics (Cambridge). Pioneer in applying Sparse Autoencoders to vision models.
